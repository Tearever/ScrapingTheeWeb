Headline:
      Lance Reddick, star of ‘The Wire’ and ‘John Wick,’ dead at 60
    
 Summary: 
	Lance Reddick, known for his roles in "The Wire" and "John Wick," has died at the age of 60 from natural causes. An actor and musician, Reddick's career spanned TV shows like "Oz," "Lost," and "Fringe," as well as video game voice work. He leaves behind a wife and two children.
