Headline:
      ‘John Wick: Chapter 4’ indulges in a particular kind of sequel inflation
    
 Summary: 
	"John Wick: Chapter 4" is a lengthy, action-packed sequel that showcases impressive stunts but suffers from sequel inflation. The film adds emotional resonance due to the death of co-star Lance Reddick. While the action is excessive, the quality of supporting actors and humor help to elevate the movie, premiering in US theaters on March 24. Rated R.
