Headline:
      ‘Ted’ brings Seth MacFarlane’s foul-mouthed teddy bear back to life on Peacock
    
 Summary: 
	A prequel series on Peacock brings Seth MacFarlane's foul-mouthed teddy bear "Ted" back to life, featuring a teenage Mark Wahlberg with the talking bear as his only friend. The show is raunchy, juvenile, and occasionally funny, set in the 90s with references to the era, offering light entertainment for fans.
