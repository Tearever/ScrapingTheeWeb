Headline:
      ‘Boston Strangler’ unravels the politics and doubts around the serial-killer case
    
 Summary: 
	"Boston Strangler" dives into the politics and doubts surrounding the serial killer case of Albert DeSalvo. The film highlights the challenges faced by a female reporter investigating the murders, as well as the reluctance of authorities to fully pursue the case. Despite some dilution in focus, the enduring fascination with true crime ensures the story's relevance.
